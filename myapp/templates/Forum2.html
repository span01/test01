    Eat Period-friendly Foods

    Instead of pigging out on ice cream while binge-watching Netflix, how about treating yourself to some well-deserved dark chocolate? Dark chocolate is not only a tasty snack, but it also helps to relieve menstrual cramps! Just be sure that your dark chocolate has at least 65% or more cacao! Other period-friendly foods you can snack on include bananas, low-fat yogurt, berries , peanut butter and walnuts.
